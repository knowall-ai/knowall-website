Case study · Invoice automation

A leading property developer, 1,600 invoices a month

Challenge

The finance team manually processed around 1,600 invoices each month — sorted from a shared inbox, verified and keyed into NAV/Continia. Time-consuming, error-prone, and migration to cloud Business Central risked delays and duplicate invoices.

Solution

Power Automate and AI capture invoices via a central mailbox; flows classify and extract details with OCR, detect duplicates and route to the correct entity — connecting seamlessly with cloud Business Central, with PO validation built in.

Straight-through processing for the majority of invoices (target ≈90%)

Faster processing with fewer errors

Reliable, scalable integration with the cloud finance system

Finance self-service for attachments directly within the system

≈€50,000 8 weeks Power Automate Business Central Microsoft AI

Case study · H&S inspection

A nationwide engineering firm, paper-free inspections

Challenge

Inspectors used paper health-and-safety forms on site. Paperwork was lost and re-entered manually, creating delays and errors — missing documentation led to regulatory fines, poor visibility and unflagged defects.

Solution

A health-and-safety inspection application on the Power Platform — digital forms on tablets with photos, comments and mapping saved to Dataverse; automated validation and real-time defect notifications; Power BI insight for proactive change.

Improved compliance and reduced fines through built-in validation

Data insights and trend reporting for management visibility

Enhanced safety via real-time alerts and immediate response

Efficiency and accuracy — no manual re-entry or paper handling

≈€30,000 4 weeks Power Apps Power BI Dataverse
