Case Study: Invoice Automation

Challenge

A leading property developer's finance team manually processed around 1,600 invoices each month — sorted from a shared inbox, verified and keyed into their NAV/Continia system. The process was time-consuming, error-prone and tied to on-premises NAV. Migrating to cloud Business Central, outdated flows risked delays and duplicate invoices.

Solution

We modernised the process with Power Automate and AI. Invoices are captured via a central mailbox; flows classify and extract details with OCR, detect duplicates and route to the correct entity — connecting seamlessly with cloud Business Central. Additional logic converts PDFs to TIFF, merges multi-page documents and performs purchase-order validation. API-driven and delivered iteratively.

Benefits

  • Processes the majority of invoices without manual intervention (target ≈90%)
  • Cuts processing time and reduces errors
  • Reliable, scalable integration with the cloud finance system
  • Finance staff view and manage attachments directly within the system

Case Study: H&S Inspection

Challenge

A nationwide independent engineering firm working on telecom and highway infrastructure. Inspectors used paper-based health-and-safety forms on site. Paperwork was frequently lost and manually re-entered at the office, creating delays and errors. Missing documentation led to regulatory fines and poor visibility, while the paperwork volume made inspections slow and defects went unflagged.

Solution

We built a health-and-safety inspection application on the Microsoft Power Platform. Field engineers complete digital forms on tablets via Power Apps, capturing photos, comments and integrated mapping, saved securely in Dataverse. Power Automate workflows validate entries and trigger real-time defect notifications, while Power BI reports give real insight for proactive changes.

Benefits

  • Improved compliance and reduced fines through built-in validation
  • Data insights and trend reporting for management visibility
  • Enhanced safety via real-time alerts and immediate response
  • Efficiency and accuracy — no manual re-entry or paper handling
Cost
Circa €30,000
Timescale
4 weeks
Technologies
  • Power Apps
  • Power Automate
  • Power BI
  • SharePoint
  • Dataverse
